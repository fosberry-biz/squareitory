CUBISM

How to Play

Goal

First player to reach 10 game points wins.

Scoring Cells

  • ★★ Center — 2 pts/turn
  • ★ Middle 4 — 1 pt/turn each
  • ◈ Corner zones — +1 cube/turn

Turn (3 actions)

  • Place on your edge
  • Move stack to empty adjacent cell
  • Merge into a friendly stack
  • Battle: both sides lose equal cubes; ties destroy both

Players

  • N / E / S / W start on their edge
  • 2-player: North vs South
